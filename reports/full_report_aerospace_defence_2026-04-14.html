Intelligence Overview

Canada's Defence Industrial Strategy launched a $500B+ procurement framework on February 17 prioritizing domestic suppliers for 70% of defence acquisitions, fundamentally reshaping the competitive landscape. Canadian aerospace firms must immediately audit their supply-chain positioning and production capacity to capture this historic domestic demand wave before larger multinational competitors establish beachheads. Watch for the Defence Investment Agency legislation this spring—its implementation timeline and capitalization will determine which Canadian companies can actually scale to meet the projected $63B annual defence budget.
