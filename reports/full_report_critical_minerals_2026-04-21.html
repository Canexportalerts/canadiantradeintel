Rare Earth Elements (Yttrium)
Chinese export controls have cut US yttrium imports by 95% (333t to 17t) and driven prices up 69x since November 2025. Canadian processors and manufacturers dependent on Chinese rare earth supply face severe availability and cost risks.
ACTIONImmediately engage with the India-US critical minerals framework (May 26 deal) through Global Affairs Canada to explore alternative rare earth sourcing from India's new rare earth corridors and Quad initiative supply chains.
