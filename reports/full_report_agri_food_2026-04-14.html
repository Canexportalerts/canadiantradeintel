What This Means For YouThis represents major relief for Canadian canola farmers and exporters who faced devastating trade barriers, with industry sources reporting $1 billion removed from farm incomes. China reduced canola seed tariffs from 84% to 15% effective March 1, 2026.
This eliminates the primary cost barrier against Australian and Ukrainian suppliers, giving Canadian crushers and exporters a 4-6 week competitive window before market share consolidates.
CONFIDENCE: HIGH

Canada's pulse exports (lentils, chickpeas) face annual quota constraints; early registration captures allocation before quota saturation in late Q2.
CONFIDENCE: HIGH
SAMR has escalated pesticide residue notices targeting Canadian canola shipments as part of ongoing regulatory friction.

CONFIDENCE: MED
