Common questions

When is intelligence published?
Sector reports publish every Tuesday. Country dossiers and Canada Forward content update quarterly. Procurement signals update continuously via the Active Tender Watch hub.
Can I trial CTI before subscribing?
Yes. Email hello@canadiantradeintel.ca to request a sample report for your sector. We will send the most recent issue within one business day.
How do I cancel?
Cancel any time by emailing hello@canadiantradeintel.ca. Monthly subscribers are not billed for the following cycle. Annual subscribers receive a pro-rated credit.
Is CTI available for one sector only?
Yes. Single-sector access is available at the Subscriber price — you receive only the intelligence relevant to your sector. Contact us to set up sector-specific access.
Does CTI cover both export and import intelligence?
Yes. Every report covers both outbound (export, investment abroad) and inbound (import, FDI, supply chain) signals. CTI is not an export-only publication — it covers the full Canadian trade picture.
