CTI surfaces federal Canadian (CanadaBuys), provincial (Alberta, Saskatchewan, New Brunswick, Nova Scotia, Manitoba), municipal (Calgary), US federal (SAM.gov), EU (TED), and NATO (NSPA) tenders. BC, Quebec, and additional provincial portals coming via data partnerships.
NATO / NSPA ELIGIBILITY — Canadian companies are eligible to bid on NSPA contracts as a NATO member nation. Companies must first register in the NSPA Source File at nspa.nato.int before bidding on RFPs. FBOs (Future Business Opportunities) are advance notices — registering early gives Canadian suppliers time to get on solicitation lists before RFPs are formally issued.
