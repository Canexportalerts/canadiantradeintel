How We Work

Tier 1 High-Frequency Pipeline Feeds — monitored weekly or monthly
Statistics Canada — Canadian International Merchandise Trade (CIMT)
Monthly import/export data at the 6-digit HS commodity level. Selectable by country, province, year and month. Exportable to CSV. Our primary source for current bilateral goods trade flows across all six sectors.
Monthly · current
Statistics Canada — The Daily: International Merchandise Trade
Monthly press release covering Canada's merchandise trade with top 10 partners, NAPCS sector breakdown, and balance of payments data. We monitor via RSS — ~5 week lag from reference month.
Monthly · RSS
Statistics Canada — Canada and the World Statistics Hub
Bilateral data hub for 18 major trading partners: trade, investment, employment, and travel. Each country hub anchors our country dossier "Key Statistics" sidebars.
Annual · 18 countries
ISED — Canadian Importers Database
Company-level list of major Canadian importers by product (HS code), city, and country of origin. "Major importers" = companies accounting for up to 80% of product imports. Unique source — no equivalent exists for importer-side intelligence.
⚠️ 2023 data — most recent available; 2024 not yet released as of April 2026
2023 · CBSA-sourced
Natural Resources Canada — Mineral Trade Statistics
Bilateral mineral and metals trade data by country and commodity, classified by stage of processing (raw → refined → manufactured). Derived from CBSA/StatCan data, annotated by NRCan mineral type. Current through April 2025.
Annual · Apr 2025
NRCan — Critical Minerals Strategy Progress Update
Live government policy page covering G7 Critical Minerals Action Plan, bilateral agreements, Investment Canada Act amendments, stockpiling initiatives, and the Global Partnerships Initiative. Updated February 2026.
Feb 2026 · live
Global Affairs Canada — Canada's State of Trade 2025
Annual comprehensive report on Canada's international trade performance. Covers merchandise exports/imports, services trade, FDI flows, sector-by-sector performance, and economic outlook. 2025 edition covers full-year 2024 data.
Annual · 2025
Global Affairs Canada — Canada's Response to U.S. Tariffs
Live policy page covering tariff relief measures, support programs, retaliatory measures, and sector impacts. Actively updated as developments occur — a standing cross-sector risk signal in all six of our reports.
Active · live
CanadaBuys — Government of Canada Procurement
Official federal procurement portal. Covers tender notices, awarded contracts, and standing offers. Key source for our Procurement Monitor — we surface the top opportunities in Aerospace & Defence, Technology, and Manufacturing weekly.
Daily · RSS
Tier 2 Industry & Crown Corporation Sources — sector reports and dossiers
EDC — Canada's Cleantech Future: Scalable, Profitable, Essential (2025)
EDC's flagship annual cleantech report. Key finding: 77% of Canada's cleantech exports go to the U.S., creating significant concentration risk as trade tensions persist. Identifies renewable energy, EVs, and electrification as Canada's three core export advantages.
🔐 Full report requires free EDC account registration
Oct 2025 · annual
EDC — Country Risk & Market Intelligence
Per-country risk ratings, market articles, and trade intelligence for 200+ markets. EDC's risk assessments inform the risk scores in each CTI country dossier, and their country articles feed our "Further Reading" recommendations.
Continuous
EDC — India Export Opportunities for Canadian Businesses
EDC's market intelligence on India's priority sectors (agri-food, advanced manufacturing, clean tech, digital, critical minerals) and EDC's 20-year in-market presence. Informs our India country dossier and four sector reports.
Nov 2025
IEA — Global Critical Minerals Outlook 2025
International benchmark for critical minerals supply/demand projections, investment trends, and policy mechanisms for supply chain diversification. We use this as the international context layer for our Critical Minerals sector reports.
2025 · annual
World Bank — Projects & Operations (Procurement)
Global development bank project database. Canadian companies in infrastructure, engineering, agri-food, and cleantech can bid on World Bank-financed contracts worldwide. We surface these opportunities in our Procurement Monitor.
Continuous
BDC — Market Research & Business Intelligence
BDC publishes sector outlooks, SME survey results, and market intelligence for Canadian small and medium businesses. Quarterly SME surveys provide cross-sector business sentiment signals that appear in our Zone A cross-sector briefings.
Quarterly

A note on importer company data: The ISED Canadian Importers Database is the only government source that identifies which specific companies are importing which products into Canada. The most recent release uses 2023 data (compiled from CBSA customs records). ISED typically releases this data with an approximately two-year lag. We expect 2024 data to become available later in 2026. For current aggregate import volumes by product and country (without company names), we supplement with StatCan CIMT, which updates monthly. We note the data vintage clearly wherever importer company data appears.
