· Uk Offshore Wind Immediate
UK launches £300 million Great British Energy Supply Chain Fund targeting offshore wind component manufacturing with fir
GBE Supply Chain Fund opened December 11, 2025 with £300 million available for UK manufacturing of constrained offshore wind components including blades, turbines, transmission cables and converter stations. Fund targets critical supply chain bottlenecks and expects to mobilize over £1 billion in public-private investment through 2030. Canadian steel tariffs create potential advantage for domestic offshore wind manufacturing as lead times for steel components run 5-7 years.
What This Means For YouMixed implications for Canadian businesses. Opportunity for Canadian companies to establish UK manufacturing partnerships or joint ventures to access fund benefits. Risk for Canadian component exporters as fund aims to build domestic UK capacity. Canadian steel producers may benefit from 25% import tariffs creating competitive advantage over international suppliers in offshore wind supply chains.

· South Korea Clean Energy This Quarter
South Korea commits to $100 billion US energy purchases including LNG amid trade uncertainty
As of February 2026, South Korea has committed to purchasing approximately $100 billion worth of energy from the US, including LNG, though uncertainty persists over whether the agreement will proceed given indications the US could increase reciprocal tariffs from 15% to 25%. The preliminary 12th Basic Plan for Electricity Supply and Demand, expected to include detailed energy transition plans and hydrogen/ammonia co-firing guidance, will likely be released in the first half of 2026.
What This Means For YouThis massive LNG commitment creates competitive pressure for Canadian LNG exporters and potential opportunities for Canadian cleantech companies in South Korea's energy transition. Canadian companies should prepare for increased competition in South Korean markets while exploring partnerships in hydrogen and co-firing technologies that will be detailed in the upcoming 12th BPLE.
